what is bullying

bullying is unwanted,aggressive behavior that insolves a real or perceived power imbalance.it can include physical,verbal,or online(cyber bullying) actions that hurt,humiliate,or intimidate others

effects of bullying

  • low self esteem
  • anxiety and depression
  • poor academic performance
  • long-term emotional damage

prevention

here are some ways to prevent bullying

  1. educate yourself and others about bullying
  2. encourage open communication and listening
  3. report bullying incidents to authorities or school officials
  4. support victms and create a safe environment
